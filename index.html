Crystal Game

Rules

  • You will be given a random number from the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.
  • You will win the game by matching your total score to the current number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the current number
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until your click on it.
  • Each time the grame starts, the game will change the values of each cyrtal
